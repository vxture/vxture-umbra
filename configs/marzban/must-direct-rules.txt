# TeamViewer. Remote-control traffic uses TeamViewer's own protocol over port
# 5938 (TCP/UDP) with 443/80 fallback, reaching master and router servers under

# and degrades sessions to slow relay mode, so keep the whole suffix direct.

# serves client downloads/assets; dyngate.com is the legacy relay domain.
